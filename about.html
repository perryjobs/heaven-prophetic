About Us

Heaven Prophetic Deliverance Ministry is a Spirit-filled church founded to bring healing, restoration, and prophetic guidance to God’s people.

We are called to raise a prophetic generation, walking in the truth and fire of the Holy Spirit. Our ministry is grounded in prayer, worship, and deliverance, serving individuals and families who seek spiritual breakthrough.

Our Motto

"Winning the lost at the cost of my life."

Our Mission

To lead people into a powerful, life-changing relationship with Jesus Christ through the prophetic word, deliverance, and kingdom teaching.

Our Vision

To empower and equip believers to live victorious lives and impact their communities for God’s glory.
